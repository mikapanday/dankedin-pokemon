## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG, WebP, AVIF, or JPEG (PNG with transparency preferred)
- **Recommended Size**: 200x200px to 400x400px
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) works best
- **Background**: Transparent or white background recommended
